Anvi Social · The Terrace

A home for connection, culture & celebration.

The top-floor glasshouse where the city comes together.

Anvi Social is our enclosed rooftop terrace — a standout bar at its centre, a DJ booth and large LED stage, VIP booths and a flexible layout that transforms from a polished corporate breakfast to the loudest table in Lagos after dark.

Dine downstairs, host upstairs. Whatever the occasion, Social is built to hold it.

What you can host

Corporate & Networking

Product launches, brand activations, breakfasts, panels and networking socials — with AV, LED and a team that runs the room.

Private Celebrations

Birthdays, anniversaries and milestones. VIP booths, bespoke menus and a bar programme built for the occasion.

Culture & Nightlife

DJ sets, sound sessions, live music, screenings of the big matches — the terrace, till late.

The Room

Engineered for the moment.

A centrepiece bar. An LED wall that sets the scene. A DJ booth and stage for live sets and speakers alike. Enclosed glass that keeps the terrace year-round. It’s a blank canvas with world-class bones — and a team to make it sing.
