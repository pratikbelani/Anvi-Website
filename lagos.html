Refined Indo-Asian, made for sharing.

A curated sharing menu that travels from North Indian clay-oven kebabs and slow-built curries to dim sum, wok and Indo-Chinese classics.

Plates arrive to be passed, torn and shared. Downstairs is warm, elegant and all-ages; upstairs the lights drop, the bar takes over, and the private dining room closes its door on the city.

Ground floor · all ages

The Dining Room

Seventy-two covers of booths and family tables under warm, adjustable light — marble underfoot, brass inlays at the door, and a room that hums rather than roars. Elegant, but never precious: this is the floor for long dinners, birthdays that start early, and children who fall asleep in the booth.

The kitchen runs an open pass, so the tandoor's glow is part of the room.

First floor · 18+

The Bar & Dining

Dinner continues upstairs, around a central bar in polished stone and brushed metal, its shelves backlit like a jewellery case. This is where the signature cocktails are built — Asian and Indian flavours through a classic lens — and where the evening slows down on purpose.

Curated music, low light, and service that notices your glass before you do.
