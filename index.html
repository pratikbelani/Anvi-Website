Not another restaurant. A destination.

A modern hospitality destination where refined Indian cuisine meets contemporary Asian influence — built on a curated menu, elevated design and meaningful social experiences.

Anvi comes from the Sanskrit for connected — one who brings people together. Set across three floors in the heart of Victoria Island, the house moves the way an evening does: an unhurried dinner downstairs, a slow cocktail at the first-floor bar, and the city's best nights up in the Social House.

The kitchen is arranged by craft — the dim-sum steamers, the tandoor-robata wall of coal, the slow fires and the wok line — and every plate arrives to be passed, torn and shared. We sell the experience first and the cuisine second; the table simply makes the introductions.
