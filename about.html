01 — The name

We named the house after the reason it exists.

Anvi comes from the Sanskrit for connected — one who brings people together. Everything else follows from that.

Lagos already knows how to celebrate. What it was missing was a single address where an unhurried family dinner, a slow cocktail and the island's best night out could happen under one roof — where Indian and Pan-Asian traditions could sit at the same table, and where the room itself does the introducing.

Anvi Hospitality was founded in April 2025 by the Belani family to build exactly that: not another restaurant, but a modern hospitality destination that sells the experience first and the cuisine second.

02 — The intent

To establish Anvi as the premier destination for innovative dining and entertainment in Lagos — blending Indian and Pan-Asian influences with global trends.

The vision

To create a versatile space where exceptional food, timeless design and vibrant entertainment converge — an experience that bridges cultures and generations.

04 — The design

Warm, dark and tactile.

The palette is drawn from the things the kitchen touches — saffron burgundy, ivory stone, sandalwood, kohl. Oxblood leather against cream plaster; marble underfoot; brass at the door; a chandelier that behaves like a slow firework.

Look closer and the details repeat: hand-drawn checkerboards, terracotta diamonds and the peacock — our sign that the evening has somewhere to strut.

The peacock walks both worlds.

India's national bird, at home in a Lagos garden — proud, unhurried and impossible to ignore. It marks our signatures on the menu and our best nights on the calendar.
