A home for connection, culture & celebration.

The glasshouse above the island — retractable glass walls, greenery and mirrored light, and a room that transforms into whatever the night demands.

By day it hosts launches, screenings and corporate networking with the skyline as a backdrop. By night the LED stage wakes up, the selectors take the booth, and the Social House becomes the reason people cancel their next-morning plans.
