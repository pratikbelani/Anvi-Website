Anvi · Sanskrit for “connected”

A modern hospitality destination where refined Indian cuisine meets contemporary Asian influence.

From the first bite to the last sip, every moment at Anvi is curated for indulgence, connection and celebration.

Set across three floors in the heart of Victoria Island, Anvi is not another restaurant — it is a destination. A curated sharing menu, signature cocktails, elevated design and a social terrace made for the city’s best nights. We sell the experience first, and the cuisine second.

Anvi Lagos · The Table

Refined Indo-Asian, made for sharing.

A curated menu that travels from North Indian clay-oven kebabs and slow-built curries to Pan-Asian dim sum, wok and robata. Built around the table — plates arrive to be passed, torn and shared.

Our kitchen leads with a handful of signatures you’ll come back for: the Anvi Prawns, a saffron-rich Prawn Malai Curry, and dim sum folded to order.
