Anvi Lagos · Ground & First Floor

Refined Indo-Asian dining.

Where North Indian tradition meets the Pan-Asian table.

Anvi Lagos is built around the shared table. Our kitchen draws from two great culinary traditions — the clay ovens and slow-built curries of North India, and the wok, robata and dim sum of Pan-Asia — and plates them to be passed and enjoyed together.

This is fine dining without the formality: precise, generous and warm. Come hungry, order broadly, and let the table fill.

Ground Floor

The Dining Room

Warm, welcoming and made for all ages. The ground floor is where families gather and friends linger over long, unhurried dinners — understated elegance, considered service, and a menu that rewards sharing.

First Floor · 18+

The Lounge, Bar & Private Dining

Upstairs turns more intimate. A central bar pours signature cocktails inspired by Indian and Pan-Asian flavours, lounge seating invites you to settle in, and private dining suites — with collapsible doors — flex from an intimate dinner to a celebration of forty.

The Kitchen

Cooked with intent, plated with care.

Every dish begins with the finest ingredients and a respect for its roots. Spices are roasted and ground in-house; breads are pulled to order from the tandoor; dumplings are folded through service. Our signatures — the Anvi Prawns, Butter Chicken, the 24K Raan Peshawari — are the ones you’ll plan your next visit around.
