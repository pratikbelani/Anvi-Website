Our Story

Where cultures connect.

The Name

Anvi — from the Sanskrit for “connected”; one who brings people together.

That single idea runs through everything we do.

Anvi is a hospitality brand that blends timeless design, vibrant entertainment and world-class cuisine. Spanning three floors in the heart of Victoria Island, it is a place where culture, elegance and energy converge — and where a meal becomes a memory. We reimagine dining and entertainment as one continuous experience: refined Indian cuisine, contemporary Asian influence, and a social terrace made for the city’s best nights.

Vision

To be Lagos’ leading destination for culinary excellence and immersive entertainment — bridging diverse cultures and creating lasting memories for our guests.

Mission

To create a harmonious space where people come together to celebrate flavours, design and experiences — the rich traditions of Indian and Pan-Asian cultures, infused with modern sophistication.

Design

Warm, dark and tactile.

Our interiors marry timeless aesthetics with cultural richness — oxblood and maroon, cream and terracotta, marble, brass and natural wood, softened by greenery and moody, shifting light. Every surface is chosen to be touched, every corner to be lingered in.

It is a space that transforms with the hours: understated by day, cinematic by night.

The Peacock

Opulence, pride, beauty, abundance.

Our emblem is the peacock — a symbol shared across South Asian and South-East Asian cultures, representing the very qualities we build the Anvi experience around. You’ll find it woven through our space, our patterns and our plates.

Come be part of it.
