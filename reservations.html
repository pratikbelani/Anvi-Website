House notes

Good to know, before you come.

Dress code +

Smart casual across the house — neat, presentable, effortless. For the first floor and Social House, think collars and closed toes for men, elevated ease for everyone. Sportswear, beachwear and flip-flops stay home.

Ages & floors +

The ground-floor dining room welcomes all ages — children are part of the plan, with supervision. The first floor — bar & dining — and the Social House are strictly 18+; valid ID may be requested.

Reservations & walk-ins +

Reservations are recommended, especially for weekends, the private dining room and VIP booths. Walk-ins are welcome whenever the room allows — expect a short wait at peak hours.

Smoking +

The dining floors are smoke-free. Designated smoking areas are available — ask the team and they'll point the way.

Photography +

Capture your evening — no flash, and mind the tables around you. Professional shoots and videography need prior approval; other guests should never end up in your frame without asking.

Outside food & allergies +

Outside food and drink aren't permitted, with exceptions for baby food and specific dietary needs. Tell us about allergies when you book — the kitchen takes them seriously.
