Chapter 03 — Dine

Two great traditions, one kitchen.

Our kitchen runs four stations — the tandoor's clay heat, the slow-built curry pot, the dim sum steamer and the wok's high flame. The menu travels from North Indian kebabs to Pan-Asian small plates, and every dish arrives to be passed, torn and shared.

Look for the house signatures — the Anvi Prawns, the saffron-rich Signature Prawn Malai Curry, dim sum folded to order.
